COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

Advanced Customer Service AI is committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.

1. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation):
- We comply with all GDPR requirements for EU users
- Lawful basis for data processing documented
- Data Protection Impact Assessments conducted
- Data Protection Officer appointed
- Breach notification procedures in place

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act):
- California residents' privacy rights respected
- Do Not Sell My Personal Information option available
- Annual compliance audits conducted

SOC 2 Type II:
- Security, availability, and confidentiality controls implemented
- Annual third-party audits completed
- Continuous monitoring and improvement

HIPAA (For Healthcare Customers):
- Business Associate Agreements available
- PHI handling procedures documented
- Regular HIPAA compliance training for staff

2. DATA PROTECTION STANDARDS

Encryption:
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications

Access Controls:
- Role-based access control (RBAC) implemented
- Multi-factor authentication required
- Principle of least privilege enforced
- Regular access reviews conducted

3. SECURITY STANDARDS

ISO 27001:
- Information Security Management System certified
- Risk assessment procedures established
- Incident response plans tested quarterly

NIST Cybersecurity Framework:
- Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover framework followed
- Continuous security monitoring
- Threat intelligence integration

4. INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC COMPLIANCE

PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry):
- Level 1 PCI DSS compliant for payment processing
- Quarterly vulnerability scans
- Annual penetration testing
- Secure payment gateway integration

Financial Services:
- SOX compliance for financial reporting customers
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML) procedures
- Know Your Customer (KYC) verification

5. DATA RESIDENCY AND SOVEREIGNTY

Regional Data Storage:
- EU data stored within EU data centers
- US data stored in US data centers
- Option for customers to specify data location

Cross-Border Transfers:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) implemented
- Privacy Shield alternative mechanisms
- Local data protection laws respected

6. VENDOR MANAGEMENT

Third-Party Risk Assessment:
- All vendors undergo security assessments
- Data Processing Agreements signed
- Regular vendor compliance audits
- Vendor breach notification requirements

7. EMPLOYEE COMPLIANCE

Training Requirements:
- Annual security awareness training mandatory
- Privacy and data protection training
- Compliance certification for relevant roles
- Regular phishing simulation tests

Background Checks:
- Criminal background checks for all employees
- Enhanced screening for access to sensitive data
- Ongoing monitoring programs

8. AUDIT AND MONITORING

Internal Audits:
- Quarterly compliance reviews
- Monthly security audits
- Weekly vulnerability assessments

External Audits:
- Annual SOC 2 Type II audits
- ISO 27001 surveillance audits
- PCI DSS quarterly scans

9. INCIDENT RESPONSE

Breach Notification:
- Documented incident response procedures
- 72-hour GDPR breach notification timeline
- Customer notification protocols
- Regulatory reporting procedures

10. RECORD KEEPING

- Subprocessor compliance requirements
