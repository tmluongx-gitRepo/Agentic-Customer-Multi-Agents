BILLING POLICY - REFUNDS AND RETURNS

Type: Static Policy Document

REFUND POLICY

1. General Refund Terms
- All refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the billing date
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Processing fees (3.5%) are non-refundable

2. Eligibility for Refunds
You may be eligible for a refund if:
- The service did not meet the specifications advertised
- Technical issues prevented service use for more than 48 consecutive hours
- You cancel within the 14-day trial period
- Duplicate charges occurred

3. Non-Refundable Items
- Setup fees and installation charges
- Custom development work already completed
- Third-party services and add-ons
- Promotional discounts and credits
- Subscription fees after 30 days

4. Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued for:
- Pro-rated unused time if canceling mid-cycle
- Service degradation affecting specific features
- Unused storage or user licenses

5. Refund Process
To request a refund:
1. Contact billing support at billing@advancedcustomerservice.ai
2. Provide your account ID and invoice number
3. Explain the reason for the refund request
4. Wait for review (typically 2-3 business days)
5. Receive confirmation and refund timeline

6. Chargebacks
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension
- Please contact us before initiating a chargeback
- We will work with you to resolve billing disputes

7. Annual Subscriptions
- Annual subscriptions are refundable within 30 days of purchase
- After 30 days, pro-rated refunds may be available at our discretion
- Cancellation of annual plans does not guarantee automatic refunds

8. Upgrade/Downgrade Refunds
- Downgrades are pro-rated for the remaining billing cycle
- Upgrades are charged immediately with credit for unused time
- Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle
